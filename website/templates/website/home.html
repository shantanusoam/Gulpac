Why Choose GLUPAC?

Industry-leading gluing and packaging solutions engineered to prevent leakage, deter tampering, and maximize day-to-day packaging throughput.

Zero Leakage Sealing

Advanced high-precision gluing and hot melt sealing technology secures complete package structural integrity for food and cosmetics.

Theft Resistant Systems

Secure bonding design prevents product theft or tampering throughout regional distribution and complex retail supply chain operations.

Multi-Size Compatibility

Highly adjustable and quick-change dynamic guidelines enable one single machinery unit to process multi-size carton cartons smoothly.

Industry Customization

Tailored engineering processes built uniquely to satisfy standards in food processing, retail pharma cleanrooms, cosmetics, and FMCG.

Precision Automation

High-speed automated workflows powered by state-of-the-art PLC computers and sensors guarantees consistent 85+ box output speeds.

Reduced Product Wastage

Optimized automated gluing nozzles minimize adhesive consumption and material waste, and prevent thermal tearing.
