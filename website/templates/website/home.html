About Glupac

Leading Manufacturer of Packaging Solutions

Glupac, under Suresh Raj Industries, is a pioneering manufacturer of precision gluing and packaging machinery. With installations across 5 countries and 20+ industries, we deliver customized solutions that ensure product safety, reduce wastage, and prevent supply chain theft.
