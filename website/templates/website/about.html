About Our Company

Pioneering packaging development, precision bonding technology, and custom engineering since 2006.

Pioneering Packaging Excellence

Glupac, under the banner of Suresh Raj Industries, has built its reputation as a leading manufacturer of premium, high-speed automated packaging and hot melt adhesive machinery.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to revolutionize regional cartoning operations by engineering systems that eliminate human error, guarantee leak-free packages, and significantly reduce material wastage.

Every machinery unit that leaves our production floor is a testament to rigorous quality assurance, premium structural materials, and meticulous customization tailored to our industrial client requirements.

Our Mission

To empower global industries with state-of-the-art, customized gluing and packaging automation. We commit to continuous technological enhancement, ensuring zero defect rates, high operator safety, and exceptional long-term machinery value.

Our Vision

To be internationally recognized as the benchmark of excellence in structural packaging systems. We strive to pioneer intelligence and PLC capabilities, helping modern manufacturing lines transition cleanly onto green, low-waste automated solutions.

Our Core Commitments

Our core values drive how we build machines and support clients

Zero Leakage Guarantee

Our hot melt sealing controls are optimized to create tight, seamless box bonding preventing outer content seepage under high impact.

Theft Resistant Design

Tamper-proof closure alignment guarantees that any shipping carton cannot be easily opened and reclosed without visible marking.

100% Customization

Every factory floor space demands custom design. We scale width, height, nozzle points, speed, and safety shields to exact client specifications.

Automation Excellence

We integrate with top-tier electronic providers like Schneider and Delta, running PLC intelligence to automatically monitor line faults.

Expert Technical Support

Transitioning to automation requires support. We provide comprehensive onsite training, documentation, and instant remote assistance.

Quality Assurance

Before packing, every single machine undergoes a non-stop 48-hour continuous cycle test with full carton load to verify electronic integrity.
